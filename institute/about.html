About Dnyan Sanskar Pre School

Dnyan Sanskar Pre School

Develop foundational skills like counting, identifying shapes, colors, and letters, as well as socializing, following simple instructions, and expressing themselves through art and play.

Classes are:

1. **Nursery Class:**
- Age: Usually for children aged 2 to 3.
- Focus: Introduction to a structured learning environment, socialization, basic concepts like colors, shapes, and numbers, through play-based activities.

2. **Playgroup:**
- Age: Children around 3 to 4 years old.
- Focus: Building social skills, fostering creativity, basic language development, and further exploration of foundational concepts through interactive and imaginative play.

3. **Lower Kindergarten (LKG):**
- Age: Typically for 4 to 5-year-olds.
- Focus: Introduction to more formal learning, basic literacy skills (letters, phonics), numeracy skills (counting, simple math), refining motor skills, and nurturing cognitive development through structured activities.

4. **Upper Kindergarten (UKG):**
- Age: Usually for children aged 5 to 6.
- Focus: Preparation for primary education, further development of literacy and numeracy skills, enhanced social interaction, and cognitive growth through more advanced activities and educational games.

This sequence provides a gradual progression from a more play-oriented and exploratory learning environment to a more structured and academically focused setting, aligning with the developmental stages of young children.
